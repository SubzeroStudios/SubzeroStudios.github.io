What Makes Her A Leader?

A leader is a person or thing that leads. Leaders guide people in the direction that they believe. TO be a leader, someone must be able to stick up for what they believe in, and cannot be fazed by what is said about them. Malala wrote for education, and when she was threatened, she continued to write, and even when she was shot at, she still continued to write, because she believes in having an education
